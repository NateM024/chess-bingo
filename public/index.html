How to play
Two ways to win
01
Create or join a game
One player creates a room and shares the 6-character code. The other player enters it to join. Each player gets their own randomly generated bingo card.
02
Play chess normally
Make your moves on the chess board. Both players see the same game. Play to win by checkmate — but keep one eye on your bingo card.
03
Bingo squares auto-fill
As events happen on the board — a pawn promotes, a knight forks two pieces, en passant is played — matching squares on both players' bingo cards fill in automatically.
04
First to win, wins
Complete a row, column, or diagonal on your bingo card before your opponent does — or deliver checkmate. Either path wins the game.
